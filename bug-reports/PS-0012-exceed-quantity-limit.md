## PS-0012 — System allows adding more than 5 identical products to the cart

**Module:** Cart / Business Logic  
**Severity:** Critical  
**Priority:** High  
**Environment:** Windows 11, Chrome (latest), PetStore Demo

### Preconditions
User is logged in.

### Steps to Reproduce
1. Open the website.
2. Log in.
3. Add more than 5 identical products to the cart.

### Expected Result
The system prevents adding more than 5 identical products.

### Actual Result
The system allows adding more than 5 identical products without restriction.
