## PS-0004 — Shopping cart is not updated after clicking [Update Cart]

**Module:** Shopping Cart  
**Severity:** Major  
**Priority:** High  
**Environment:** PetStore web application, Desktop (Chrome)

### Preconditions
User has at least one product added to the cart.

### Steps to Reproduce
1. Open the Shopping Cart page.
2. Change the quantity of a product in the cart.
3. Click the [Update Cart] button.

### Expected Result
The cart should be updated according to the changes (e.g. quantity, total price).

### Actual Result
The cart is not updated. The quantity and total price remain unchanged.
